A Turnkey Home Above the Atlantic

Move in tomorrow, or let it pay for itself.

This light-filled, 70m² apartment sits in a small, quiet complex of just four homes in Fish Hoek — one of Cape Town's most sought-after seaside villages on the False Bay coast. Floor-to-ceiling windows and a private balcony frame uninterrupted views over the ocean and the surrounding mountains, while the open-plan living area, fitted kitchen, and comfortable bedroom are finished to a high standard throughout.

The apartment is fully furnished and completely turnkey — ready to move into immediately, use as a lock-up-and-go holiday base, or rent out as an investment. Residents share a maintained pool and garden, and the complex is monitored by a security company for peace of mind. You're a short walk from Fish Hoek's beach, restaurants, and shops, with everything you need close by.
